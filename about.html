About Us

We are a Cooperative ZAMUKA, we are from BUGESERA in East RWANDA.
75% of Rwanda's agricultural production comes from smallholder farmers.
Rwanda's principal crops include coffee, pyrethrum, tea, flowers, beans, cassava banana,
Irish potatoes, rice, wheat, sugarcane, among others. About 61% of Rwandan soil is
suitable for agriculture as the soils are fertile.

Agricultural sector general information

Agriculture is the main economic activity in Rwanda with 70%
of the population engaged in the sector, and around 72%
of the working population employed in agriculture.
